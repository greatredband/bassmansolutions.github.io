Great Red

Rochester, NY

BOOKING

Alternative Rock:
Redefined

Great Red, a four piece out of Rochester, NY, is a band of hipster-dweebs eager to create. Their sound exhibits perfectly the tension and passion that stems from each member itching to squeeze music in every possible remaining crevice of time after family, work and sleep have been conquered.

The genre Great Red presents is a subtle mix of indie and alternative rock. Each member brings a unique sound to the mix, however.

With influences ranging from funk and classic rock to jazz and experimental rock, this band of suburban origins is ready to show that small cities have no problems keeping up with the times.

The Set

The current time for a Great Red set is 45 minutes.

The band has been together since August of 2016, and has crafted eight originals since.
